Your trusted partner for metrology, calibration, and precision measurement systems in Indonesia. Official agent for world-leading brands.

About Us

PT Metrologi Presisi Utama presents the ultimate services in the field of Metrology and Measurement Systems. Our special services include supplying all types of dimensional metrology equipment and instruments, calibration, upgrading and refurbishing, and much more. Our company is currently staffed by 4 persons with more than 20 years of hands-on experience in the field of metrology and are fully trained. This ensures that any of your inquiries will be handled accurately, efficiently, and competitively. Regular training for all staff is comprehensively programmed throughout the year to ensure the best understanding of the latest technology and future trends.

With our individual experience, we have proven our integrity and high level of service to our customers in Indonesia. Service is the main emphasis of our company business, although implementation of the highest available technology will always be the main consideration in each case. The best service can only be provided to you through our intensive and direct contact with all of our principals. This delivers fast and quick responses to any of your problems and questions. We are fully supported by our principals and have been appointed as the agent of their products in the territory of Indonesia. The trust and cooperative relations bring the best benefits to all our customers.

Our Activities

  • Supplying a comprehensive range of measuring instruments and machines, from simple metrology equipment up to coordinate measuring machines with the latest electronics, software, and accessories.
  • Retrofit of old measuring machines with the latest software, computer systems, electronics, controllers, probing systems, positioning scales, and encoders. Don’t throw away your old machines—they may be feasible for retrofit!
  • Calibration services in the fields of dimensional metrology, temperature devices, force and pressure devices, and electric equipment. These devices are commonly used in automotive, transportation, machinery, mould and dies, general construction, heavy industries, and many others.
  • Marketing of second-hand CMM machines. Each machine is fully refurbished and retrofitted using current electronic equipment, controls, computers, and software. All second-hand machines are in “ready to use” condition and come with a 1-year warranty.
  • Training in operation procedures of measuring devices and instruments, covering installation, setup, repair, adjustment, and maintenance.
  • Design, manufacture, and sourcing of special/non-standard measuring systems, gauges, and other devices for fast measurement of mass production items and to provide feedback for automatic adjustment control.
